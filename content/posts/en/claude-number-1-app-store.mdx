## Claude Is the #1 App — on Both iOS and Android

On February 28, 2026, the **Claude by Anthropic** app hit #1 on the U.S. Apple App Store for the first time in history, overtaking ChatGPT — which had held the top spot all month.

Days later, Claude reached #1 on Google Play as well — both in Productivity and overall.

Numbers from Sensor Tower:
- Claude jumped from **#131** (Jan) to **Top 20** (Feb) to **#1** in 3 days
- Claude downloads surged **51%**
- ChatGPT uninstalls jumped **295%**
- Anthropic said "every day this week set an all-time record for sign-ups"
- Free users grew over **60%** since January
- Paid subscribers **doubled** within the year

## What Happened? — The Pentagon Drama

It all started with negotiations between Anthropic and the U.S. Department of Defense (Pentagon), which had been using Claude as its sole AI model.

**Dario Amodei** (Anthropic's CEO) drew two red lines:

1. **No using Claude for autonomous weapons systems**
2. **No using Claude for mass domestic surveillance**

The Pentagon pushed back. **Pete Hegseth** (Secretary of Defense) ordered Anthropic classified as a "supply-chain risk to national security" — the same designation as Huawei.

**Trump** posted on Truth Social calling it a "DISASTROUS MISTAKE."

Then **OpenAI stepped in** — Sam Altman announced a deal with the Pentagon within hours, claiming "similar safeguards for surveillance and autonomous weapons."
